Overview

Slide is a very simple puzzle game I created while learning C# and Unity3D over the course of 8 months. The goal is to swipe in a direction to move and try and reach the green tile. The catch is you will continue to slide until you hit a wall. Some of the major features included 140 levels which were personally designed, and a range of mechanics such as portals and interchanging tiles.

I was particularly proud of this project as it was the first major software engineering project I had ever completed to such a high standard. Almost no issues were reported on release and the app received highly positive reviews and comments on the app store and social media platforms.
